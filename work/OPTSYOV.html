one point to see your own views
Acrylic, Speaker, Diffuser, DC Fan, Program
2017

one point to see your own view is a type of storytelling device and system. The device creates a multimodal phenomenon by stimulating the sense of sound, smell, and touch. Through this experience, we try to create a new type of vr experience to allow the audience to participate.
