Mamoru Watanabe is a London based artist born in 1992, Tokyo. His main interest relies on the relationship between technology and humans. He working with multimedia and collaborative practice. Aiming attention on the human perception, Mamoru's work across a wide range of media in the form of audiovisual, video, sculpture, and installation.
